About the Mental Health Chatbot

The Mental Health Chatbot is an AI-powered tool designed to provide emotional support, mental health resources, and guidance. Whether you're feeling stressed, anxious, or just need someone to talk to, our chatbot is here to help. It offers a safe and confidential space to express your feelings, learn coping strategies, and connect with professional resources. Our goal is to make mental health support accessible to everyone, anytime, anywhere.

Mental health is just as important as physical health, and we believe that everyone deserves access to the tools and support they need to thrive. Our chatbot is not a replacement for professional therapy, but it can be a helpful companion on your journey toward better mental well-being.
